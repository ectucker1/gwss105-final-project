Queer Legal Activism

There are many visualizations of the current wave of anti-LGBT legislation, such as this one from the ACLU. These capture important information and are a great tool to stay informed and strategize around legal attacks.

But just seeing the ant-queer legislation shows only part of the story. Resisting with these laws, there have been a wide variety of lawsuits and other actions by organizations such as the ACLU and Lambda Legal.
